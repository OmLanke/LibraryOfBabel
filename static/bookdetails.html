Dr. Lal Krishnan

Arogya Kalpadruma

4 Reviews

In summary, "Arogya Kalpadruma" has received mixed reviews from readers. Some found it to be a life-changing and valuable resource for holistic health, praising its wealth of information and thoughtful presentation. Others, however, were disappointed, noting that the book lacked depth, practicality, and scientific support. It was seen as a decent introduction to holistic health for newcomers, but not worth the investment for those seeking more comprehensive and evidence-based guidance. Overall, opinions on the book's value varied, with some readers finding it highly beneficial while others were left wanting more.
